## 1. Brand Identity Core

**Brand Name:** CleanMateX  
**Tagline:** Smart. Fast. Spotless. (Arabic: ذكي. سريع. نقي.)  
**Mission:** To digitize, automate, and elevate every aspect of laundry and dry-cleaning operations through smart, bilingual, AI-driven SaaS.  
**Vision:** To be the leading GCC-first laundry management platform—trusted by small shops and large franchises alike.  
**Values:** Innovation, Reliability, Simplicity, Customer Focus, GCC Localization.  
**Voice & Tone:** Clean, professional, bilingual, friendly but precise.  

**Friendly but Precise:** Achieved through clear, actionable UI text, empathetic support language, and educational marketing copy.  

**Brand Story:** CleanMateX was founded to empower laundry operators with tools once only available to large enterprises. It elevates everyday operations through digitization, offering GCC-standard efficiency, clarity, and growth potential.

---

## 2. Visual Identity System

**Primary Logo:** CleanMateX with stylized “X” representing motion, precision, and circular efficiency.  
**Color Versions:** Full color (preferred), white on dark, and monochrome (for print).  

**Logo Concept for X:**  
- **Motion Flow:** Overlapping arrows resembling a washer drum rotation.  
- **Spotless:** Subtle sparkle gradient.  
- **Precision:** Folded fabric forming a clean, sharp X.  

**Logo Lockup:** Tagline centered below with smaller font weight.  
**Clear Space:** X-height margin around all sides.  
**Minimum Size:** 24px digital / 10mm print.  

**Color Palette:**  
Primary: Aqua Blue `#0AB6D8`  
Secondary: Deep Navy `#0E2A47`  
Accent: Emerald Green `#1DBF73`  
Alert/Action: Amber `#FFB100`  
Success: Teal `#14B8A6`  
Error: Coral Red `#E44D4D`  
Neutral: `#F8FAFC`, `#E2E8F0`, and Slate Gray `#64748B`  

**Gradients:** Aqua → Navy → Emerald for hero and splash screens.

---

## 3. Typography

**English:** Inter (body), Poppins (headings)  
**Arabic:** Tajawal (body), Cairo (headings)  
**Print:** Noto Sans / Noto Naskh Arabic  

**Font Hierarchy:** Headings 700, Body 400, Captions 300  
**Line-height:** 1.5 body / 1.3 headings  
**Letter-spacing:** 0.5% for headings  
**Fallbacks:** Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif  

Use Inter for readability and Poppins for brand touchpoints.

---

## 4. Iconography & Illustrations

**Icon Style:** Outline, rounded (12px radius), uniform stroke.  
**Libraries:** Lucide/Feather for web; Material for mobile.  
**Custom Icon Plan:** Create unique icons (laundry basket, hanger, delivery van, AI symbol) with subtle X references.  
**Illustration Style:** Minimalist, line-art, flat, brand colors only. Include diversity and cultural sensitivity.

---

## 5. UI/UX Branding System

**Grid:** 8px  
**Corner Radius:** 12px  
**Shadow:** shadow-md  
**Primary Button:** Aqua Blue `#0AB6D8` (hover darken 10%, active 20%)  
**Secondary Button:** Navy border → fill Aqua on hover  
**Inputs:**  
- Default: Border `#E2E8F0`  
- Focus: Aqua border  
- Error: Coral border  
- Disabled: Light gray background  

**Modals:** 80% overlay, title Poppins bold, right-aligned buttons.  
**Loader/Spinner:** Rotating “X” gradient (Aqua → Emerald).  
**Theme:** Light and Dark (auto detection).

---

## 6. Bilingual & Cultural Design

**Languages:** EN/AR with full RTL mirroring.  
**Localization:** Date, time, and currency per GCC region.  
**Numbers:** Arabic or Western numerals per locale.  
**Cultural Guidance:** Respectful, inclusive visuals and attire norms. Avoid cultural clichés.

---

## 7. Brand Usage Scenarios

- **App Icon:** X mark on Aqua background.  
- **Website Header:** Full logo + tagline.  
- **Admin Portal:** Icon-only sidebar; full logo on login.  
- **Splash Screen:** Aqua → Navy gradient.  
- **Invoices:** Bilingual header, Arabic right-aligned.  
- **Email:** White background, Aqua CTA button, clean layout.  
- **Social Media:** Use logo_icon.svg on solid brand color.
