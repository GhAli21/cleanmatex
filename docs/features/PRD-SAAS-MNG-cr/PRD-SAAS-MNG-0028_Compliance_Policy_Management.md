# PRD-SAAS-MNG-0028: Compliance & Policy Management

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Owner:** Jehad Ali (Jh Apps HQ)

## Overview & Purpose

This PRD defines the compliance and policy management system for GDPR, data protection regulations, and policy enforcement.

**Business Value:**
- Regulatory compliance
- Data protection
- Policy enforcement
- Compliance reporting
- Risk mitigation

---

## Functional Requirements

### FR-COMP-001: GDPR Compliance
- **Description**: GDPR compliance management
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Data subject rights
  - Right to be forgotten
  - Data portability
  - Consent management
  - GDPR reporting

### FR-COMP-002: Data Retention Policies
- **Description**: Enforce data retention policies
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Define retention policies
  - Automated data deletion
  - Retention policy enforcement
  - Retention reports

### FR-COMP-003: Privacy Policy Management
- **Description**: Manage privacy policies
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Policy versioning
  - Policy publishing
  - Policy acceptance tracking
  - Policy updates

### FR-COMP-004: Terms of Service Management
- **Description**: Manage terms of service
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - ToS versioning
  - ToS publishing
  - ToS acceptance tracking
  - ToS updates

### FR-COMP-005: Compliance Audit Trails
- **Description**: Compliance audit logging
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Log compliance actions
  - Compliance reports
  - Audit trail export
  - Compliance dashboards

### FR-COMP-006: Data Deletion Workflows
- **Description**: Data deletion workflows
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Right to be forgotten workflow
  - Data deletion requests
  - Deletion confirmation
  - Deletion audit trail

### FR-COMP-007: Compliance Reporting
- **Description**: Compliance reporting
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Compliance status reports
  - Compliance checklists
  - Regulatory change tracking
  - Compliance dashboards
