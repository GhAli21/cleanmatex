## Overview & Purpose

This PRD defines the advanced reporting, analytics, and billing insights system for platform-wide analytics and revenue tracking.

**Business Value:**
- Business intelligence
- Revenue insights
- Growth tracking
- Churn analysis
- Data-driven decisions

---

## Functional Requirements

### FR-REPORT-001: Platform Analytics Dashboard
- **Description**: Platform-wide analytics
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Revenue analytics
  - Growth metrics
  - Tenant analytics
  - Usage analytics
  - Trend analysis

### FR-REPORT-002: Revenue Analytics
- **Description**: Revenue tracking and analysis
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Revenue by plan
  - Revenue trends
  - Revenue forecasting
  - Revenue breakdown

### FR-REPORT-003: Churn Analysis
- **Description**: Analyze tenant churn
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Churn rate tracking
  - Churn reasons
  - Churn prediction
  - Retention strategies

### FR-REPORT-004: Custom Report Builder
- **Description**: Build custom reports
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Drag-and-drop builder
  - Multiple data sources
  - Custom visualizations
  - Report scheduling

### FR-REPORT-005: Report Export
- **Description**: Export reports
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - PDF export
  - Excel export
  - CSV export
  - Scheduled exports

### FR-REPORT-006: Billing Insights
- **Description**: Billing and invoicing insights
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Billing trends
  - Invoice tracking
  - Payment tracking
  - Billing forecasts
